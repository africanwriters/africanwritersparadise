    Jahannes Mike Mupisa

    Jahannes Mike Mupisa born in Kadoma, Zimbabwe. Resides in Mwenezi, masvingo. Mupisa is a published academic writer, novelist and poet. His works have been featured in several poetry anthologies including Speak a word / Khuluma Izwi / taura Izwi, Gwatakwata Renhetembo, Mupakwa Werwendo, Mukoko wenduri amongst others. He has co-authored several heritage projects with Secondary Book Press. He attained an hounours degree in Archaeology at Midlands State University.
